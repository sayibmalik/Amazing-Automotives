Junaid Nazir
Director, Amazing Automotives OPC Pvt. Ltd.

Junaid Nazir is an accomplished professional with over a decade of experience in road safety and transport consultancy. Holding a BTech in Automobile Engineering, Junaid has dedicated his career to enhancing road safety standards, driver training programs, and accident prevention initiatives in Jammu and Kashmir.

As the Director of Amazing Automotives OPC Pvt. Ltd., Junaid oversees a range of services, including:

  • • Road safety training programs designed to educate and empower drivers and communities.
  • • Comprehensive driver training sessions tailored for various vehicle categories.
  • • Expert accident investigation services to identify causes and implement preventative measures.
  • • Transport consultation for organizations seeking to optimize their logistics and ensure compliance with safety regulations.

Junaid's leadership reflects his passion for creating safer roads and fostering a culture of responsible driving. His work with the Kashmir Road Safety Foundation has further strengthened his commitment to public welfare, where he has successfully implemented innovative strategies to reduce road accidents and improve transportation systems.

Under his guidance, Amazing Automotives has become a trusted name in the region, known for its professionalism and impact on road safety and transport management. Junaid continues to be a driving force in advancing these critical initiatives across Jammu and Kashmir
